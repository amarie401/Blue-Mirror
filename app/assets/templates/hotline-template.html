Crisis Helplines

If you or a loved one is in immediate danger, call 911.

  • Suicide Prevention Lifeline
    The Lifeline provides 24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones, and best practices for professionals.
    1-800-273-8255 TTY 1-800-799-4889 Live chat English and Spanish

  • Crisis Textline
    Crisis Text Line is free, 24/7 support for those in crisis. Text 741741 from anywhere in the USA to text with a trained Crisis Counselor.
    Text 741741

  • NAMI
    The National Alliance on Mental Illness HelpLine is a free service that provides information, referrals and support to people living with a mental health condition, family members and caregivers, mental health providers and the public.
    1-800-950-6264

  • SAMHSA
    The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration offers information services and referrals for individuals and family members facing mental and/or substance use disorders.
    1-800-662-4357 English and Spanish

  • Boys Town
    The Boys Town National Hotline is a free hotline available to kids, teens and young adults at any time.
    800-448-3000 TTY 1-800-448-1833 English and Spanish, translation services for 140 other languages

  • National Eating Disorder hotline
    National Eating Disorders hotline provides support, resources and treatment options for yourself or a loved one.
    800-931-2237 Text "NEDA" to 741741 Live chat

  • Hopeline
    Christian based service for teenagers and young adults about whatever challenges they are facing.
    1-800-273-8255 Live chat

  • RAINN
    Hotline for those dealing with sexual assault.
    800-656-4673 Live chat

  • Postpartum Support International

    The PSI Warmline is a toll-free telephone number anyone can call to get basic information, support, and resources.
    1-800-944-4773 Weekly group chat English and Spanish

  • The Hotline
    A hotline for anyone experiencing domestic violence, seeking resources or information, or questioning unhealthy aspects of their relationship.
    1-800-799-7233 TTY 1-800-787-3224 Live chat English and Spanish, support available in over other 170 languages

  • The Trevor Project
    The Trevor Project is the leading national organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and questioning (LGBTQ) young people ages 13-24.
    866-488-7386 Text "Trevor" to 1-202-304-1200 Live chat
